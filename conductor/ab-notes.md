This specification provides a comprehensive overview of **StartUp Ecosystem** platform, designed for a coding agent to build an open multi-sided marketplace for the startup ecosystem.

---

## 1. Project Overview
A multi-sided networking and marketplace platform connecting **Founders**, **Mentors**, **Incubators**, **Accelerators** and **Investors**. 

The goal is to facilitate business connections, fundraising, mentorship, and access to startup resources.

There are multiple "Anchors" like Startup Incubators, Accelerators who operate these siloed strtup multi-sided marketplace. Idea is to create a disaggregated, open ecosytem
where such anchors can interoperate and collaborate. For example, mentors, founders and investors can register and interact with multple Anchor marketplaces, without being locked in,
or duplicating their profiles.

## 2. Core Entities & Data Model

### 2.1 Users
* **Founder:** Profile includes venture details, stage, sector, and "Challenges" posted.
* **Mentor:** Profile includes specialization, years of experience, and availability for meetings.
* **Investor:** Profile includes investment thesis, preferred sectors, stage (Seed, Series A, etc.), and ticket size.
* **Vendor:** Verified service providers offering products/tools to startups.
* **Admin:** Internal users managing moderation, events, and reports.
* **Partners:** Other Incubators and Accelerators will be able to acess via MCP and A2A integration. 

### 2.2 Domain Entities
* **Venture/Startup:** Associated with Founders; contains Pitch Decks, business stage, and sector.
* **Challenge:** A post created by a Founder seeking advice or solutions.
* **Meeting/Appointment:** A scheduled interaction between users, often integrated with a virtual video tool.
* **Event:** Webinars, pitch days, or workshops (Free or Paid).
* **Product/Resource:** Digital assets like templates, research reports, or partner vendor tools.
* **AiBizScore:** An open metric or assessment tool to gauge startup readiness, generated by AI Agent.

## 3. Key Features

### 3.1 User-Specific Modules
* **Founder Dashboard:** Post challenges, track "AiBizScore," search for mentors/investors, and access the resource library.
* **Mentor/Investor Dashboard:** Filter startups by sector/stage, manage meeting requests, and browse the "Activity Wall."
* **Marketplace:** A repository of vendor products and services specifically for startups (e.g., legal templates, SaaS discounts).

### 3.2 Interaction Tools
* **Activity Wall:** A social-style feed where founders post challenges and others provide replies/solutions.
* **Matchmaking System:** Search and filter functionality based on "Investor Preference" or "Mentor Specialization.", using Agentic framework (`agent.yaml`)
* **Native Video Meetings:** In-platform video conferencing (external tools used with multiple option), should have API/MCP integration with transcription download feature.
* **Messaging System:** Direct communication channel between connected entities. options to use email, whats app, signal and and bluesky. These will be integrated through channel service of AIGen App framework.

### 3.3 Knowledge Hub
* **Learning Center:** Video, audio, and document-based content on startup terminology and funding.
* **Research Reports:** Access to sector-specific analysis and competitive matrices.

## 4. Workflows

### 4.1 Onboarding & Verification
1.  **Registration:** User selects role (Founder/Mentor/Investor).
2.  **Profile Building:** Users provide detailed credentials (LinkedIn integration recommended).
3.  **Verification (Optional):** Admin or automated check for "Verified Vendor" or "Accredited Investor" status.

### 4.2 The "Connection" Workflow
1.  **Discovery:** Founder searches for an Investor using filters (e.g., "SaaS" + "Seed Stage").
2.  **Request:** Founder sends a meeting request or profile view.
3.  **Action:** Investor accepts/declines. If accepted, a meeting slot is booked via the integrated calendar.
4.  **Interaction:** Meeting occurs via native video; follow-up notes can be exchanged.

### 4.3 The "Challenge-Solution" Workflow
1.  **Post:** Founder posts a specific business hurdle (e.g., "Need help with GTM in SE Asia").
2.  **Engagement:** Mentors/Founders reply with advice.
3.  **Conversion:** If advice is valuable, the Founder may convert the interaction into a formal Mentorship session.

## 5. Business & Monetization Model

### 5.1 Subscription Tiers (Founders)
* **Lite (Free):**
    * Unlimited connections.
    * 3 meetings/profile views per month.
    * Access to basic learning tools.
* **Pro (Paid - e.g., ₹999/mo):**
    * Increased limit (20+ meetings).
    * Access to premium sector reports and "aiBizScore" analytics.
    * Founder-to-founder networking.

### 5.2 Mentor Monetization
* **Paid Mentorship:** Mentors can choose to charge for sessions.
* **Revenue Share:** Platform may take a commission on paid mentorship or vendor sales.
